let me introduce myself...

Former IT Manager. I have always been interested in web development. The idea that you could create a working website from scratch very much appealed to me.
Following many years at the same company, where I held various positions, I tendered my resignation and decided to pursue a career in Web Development. Though, the learning curve was steep, it was very enjoyable! I am currently working as a freelance Front End Web Developer and loving it. That said, I am available for full-time or contract work.
